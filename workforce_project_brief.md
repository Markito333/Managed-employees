# Project Brief: Workforce Management System

## 1. Vision
To provide a modern, minimalist tool for businesses to manage their most valuable asset—their people—with ease and clarity.

## 2. Design Aesthetic
*   **Style:** Minimalist, clean, and professional.
*   **Typography:** Poppins (Regular to Bold) for a friendly yet structured feel.
*   **Colors:** A neutral foundation of white and deep greys, accented by a soft pastel green (#C1E1C1) to signify action and positive status.

## 3. Core Functionality
*   **Executive Dashboard:** Real-time metrics on workforce health and upcoming events.
*   **Unified Directory:** A central hub for finding and managing employee information.
*   **Streamlined Attendance:** Frictionless clock-in/out and leave management for employees.
*   **Administrative Control:** Granular settings for organizational structure and user roles.

## 4. Current Progress
*   **Full UI Kit:** 5 core screens designed (Dashboard, Directory, Profile, Attendance, Settings).
*   **Design System:** Established tokens for colors, typography, and components.
*   **Detailed PRD:** Full technical and functional requirements documented.
